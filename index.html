    Sir Prance

    is a mobile game built on both the Android and IOS devices. We went through the entire process from downloading Android Studios and XCode to uploading the game onto the app stores. Check out Sir Prance on your device's app store. Download and play!

    Vent DJ

    is an innovative way to listen to music, allowing you to create a playlist that consists of Youtube videos and SoundCloud tracks. Start your search with your favorite songs. Add them to your queue by clicking the title, and enjoy your newly generated playlist.

    Vent Tag

    builds upon Spotify by adding hashtags to Spotify songs. This adds new ways to store and share music. Search a song, add hashtags to them, and now the songs are affiliated with that hashtag. Users can input a '#' and then the hashtag query to build a Spotify playlist with songs containing those hashtags. This was the first project Vent Origins worked on and was not the last.
